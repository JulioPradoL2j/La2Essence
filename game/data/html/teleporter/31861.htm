Watcher of Valakas Klein:
The landscape has changed significantly. The once smoldering marsh has transformed into the Fire Dragon's Swamp, a place where intense heat and ancient energy collide. Adventurers come here to test their strength against the fire-breathing creatures that have risen from the depths of the volcano.
Be cautious, for the swamp is treacherous and the heat alone can drain the life from the unprepared. Do you wish to venture further into this burning wasteland?
